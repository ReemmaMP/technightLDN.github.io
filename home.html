#technightLDN

17th March 2016, 5pm

The Shard, London

celebrating the "tech" in "diversity in tech"

About

Who is this event for?

Any student or recent graduate intersted in technology. People of all genders are welcome!

Who is running this?

We're a team of tech-loving students UCLU Tech Society, UCLU Leading Women and WarwickTECH. :)

Why are you running this?

We believe that there's so much more to women in tech, and we want to invite all genders for an insightful evening with speakers who just happen to be female.

SPEAKERS

Last year's keynote speakers were Ben Medlock, CTO of Swiftkey, and Amelia Humfress, Founder and CEO of Steer.
