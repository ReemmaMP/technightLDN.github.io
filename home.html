#technightLDN

17th March 2016, 5pm

The Shard, London

celebrating the "tech" in "diversity in tech"

About

Tech Night is a mini-conference packed with talks, workshops and networking for students and recent graduates who are curious about technology. Expect an evening of inspiration and motivation.

Who is this event for?

Any student or recent graduate interested in technology. People of all genders are welcome!

Who is running this?

We're a team of tech-loving students from UCLU TechSoc, UCLU Leading Women and WarwickTECH. :)

Why are you running this?

We believe that there's so much more to women in tech, and we want to invite all genders for an insightful evening with speakers who just happen to be female.

SPEAKERS

Last year's keynote speakers were Ben Medlock, CTO of Swiftkey, and Amelia Humfress, Founder and CEO of Steer.
